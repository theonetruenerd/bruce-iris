# Junie's Cyberpunk & Netrunner Suggestions

Welcome to the sprawl, Operator. To push the "Iris" Cyberdeck into true Netrunner territory, I've compiled a list of aesthetic and functional enhancements that blend high-tech utility with the gritty "low-life" cyberpunk vibe.

## 🎨 Aesthetic Enhancements (The "Vibe")

### 1. Retro-Futurist UI "Glitch" Effects
- **Chromatic Aberration:** Implement a slight color offset (red/blue fringing) on high-priority alerts or during "Signal Breach" events.
- **Terminal "Rain":** A subtle background animation of vertical code/hex characters (Matrix-style but in neon green or amber) when the device is idle.
- **Boot Sequence:** A fake "Kernel Loading" screen with scrolling technical jargon, checksums, and a "Secure Link Established" message before the main menu.

### 2. Physical Deck Modifications
- **Weathering (Greebling):** Scuff the edges of the Cardputer case and add "warning" stickers (e.g., "HIGH VOLTAGE", "PROPERTY OF MILITECH", "AUTHORIZED ACCESS ONLY").
- **Exposed Wiring:** Use braided cables for your peripheral attachments (I2C hub) to give it a "repaired in a back-alley" look.
- **Cyber-Deck Handle:** 3D print a ruggedized handle/shroud that protects the Cardputer and provides mounting points for the camera and NFC reader.

### 3. Lighting & Sound
- **Breathing LEDs:** Sync the Cardputer's onboard LED (and the Flora jacket) to pulse like a heartbeat ("Bio-sync") when biometric sensors are active.
- **Synthwave Alerts:** Use 8-bit or low-sample-rate audio chirps for successful hacks. Think "mechanical" and "industrial" sounds rather than clean modern beeps.

## 🛠️ Real-World Netrunner Features (The "Tech")

### 1. Digital Forensic & Stealth Tools
- **Auto-Wipe (Dead Man's Switch):** A physical button or a specific voice command ("Iris, flatline the deck") that instantly wipes temporary credentials, session logs, and history.
- **Signal Strength Warbler:** A UI element that represents WiFi/BLE signal strength as "Signal Noise" or "Interference Level." The more "noise," the closer you are to a target.
- **MAC Address "Ghosting":** Automate the randomization of MAC addresses every X minutes to prevent being tracked across public APs.

### 2. Biometric-Linked Performance
- **Stress-Responsive UI:** Link the GSR (Galvanic Stress Response) sensor to the UI color. As your stress levels rise, the screen shifts from Neon Green to Aggressive Red.
- **Heart-Rate Gated Commands:** Lock "offensive" commands (like Deauth or Jamming) until a stable heart rate is detected, simulating a "calm under pressure" requirement for the user.

### 3. Smart-Environment Interaction
- **NFC "Quick-Drop":** Tap an NFC tag at your "home base" to automatically upload all captured wardriving data to a local server.
- **IR "Ghosting":** Use the IR transmitter to "blind" or confuse modern IR-based motion sensors or cameras by flooding the area with noise.
- **Thermal "Ghost Hunting":** Use the thermal camera to detect heat signatures left on keyboards or door handles to guess PIN codes (a real-world thermal attack technique).

### 4. Wearable Integration (Iris & Flora)
- **Haptic Feedback:** Integrate a small vibration motor in the magnetic wristband. Iris can "tap" your wrist when a new node is discovered or when a breach is successful.
- **Jacket "Threat Level":** The Adafruit Flora LEDs on the jacket could change patterns based on the number of nearby WiFi/BLE devices (e.g., more "hostiles" = faster flashing).

## 🦾 Iris Persona Expansion
- **Cynical Commentary:** Have Iris provide "unsolicited" advice when battery is low: *"Power's draining. Better finish this before we both go dark."*
- **Operational Logs:** Instead of "Success," use *"Breach successful. Data harvested."* Instead of "Error," use *"Signal lost in the noise. Retrying."*

Stay sharp, Operator. The net is always watching.
